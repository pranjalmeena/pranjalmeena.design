Marigold Emoji

Designed a Marigold emoji for potential usage in both Indian and international contexts.

Aug 2017

idc

emoji design

Emoji are dominating popular culture and changing how we communicate in today’s world. One can essentially have a whole conversation with nothing but Emoji. Emoji can add personality to your UI design, enabling users (and designers too!) to express themselves in an emotive and creative way. Designing an Emoji was a very interesting project because it involved multiple scenarios, options and outcomes, based on the decisions taken at any point in the design process. Conveying the intended meaning was as important as not being too vague to potentially use all possible meanings.

Why Marigolds?

Marigolds are upholders in gardens all over the world because of their luminous appearance. It is a native to North and South America. Throughout history, they have had an important spiritual and cultural significance for many different cultures. One of the major utilisation of this flower is ornamentation in various parts of the world. It also has vital medicinal and nutritional properties.

What can it be used for?

To show activities such as worship, festivals, celebrations, etc.
To communicate socio-cultural activities such as marriages, pujas etc.
To communicate belief, honour, happiness, auspiciousness and positivity
To compare the energy and glow such as, "Your skin glows like Marigold"

The Process

The flower was studied from various profiles to understand the intricate form and petal pattern, and to determine the best view to make into an Emoji.
