Schematic Map

Made a standardised schematic map of Ahmedabad, after geographical analysis and multiple levels of abstraction.

Aug 2018

idc

map design

This was an interesting project as it involved visual representation of a physical space. The process might seem like an easy one but it involved a lot of thought as to what details should it entail and to what extent. The idea is to help users capture a basic structure of the network than to show minute details.

The process was quite iterative. I started by observing the map of Ahmedabad and traced it to get a better idea of the important roads connecting the city. After getting a basic idea of how the roads are I tried observing patterns and shapes in the traced version. I abstracted the actual roads to something simpler.

Rough Visualisation of the Map of Ahmedabad.

The next step was grouping similar features and simplifying them. This part of the process included spatial arrangement and balance, generalisation of the geometry and creating a visual hierarchy. This helped me in taking the user through a journey which allows them to unfold the information in layers and not all at once.

Simplifying and grouping elements of the Map.

The last part was to create balance in all the visual elements through fonts, colour and scale. The final design includes contrast, repetition, alignment and proximity principles.

Final Map Design with all details.
